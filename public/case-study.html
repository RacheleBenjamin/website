Study 1 · Finding
Finding

Users would click 'Allow' without knowing what it means.

😬Unsure what's included in their profile

They assumed email, location, and browsing history would be included — but not financial information.

😬Weak mental model of ‘browse for you’

Users don't know what happens after clicking ‘Allow.’

Scroll or hover to reveal the insight↓

Insight
Agentic AI is unfamiliar, so users need clarity: what actions the product takes and exactly what personal data is used.
12
Study 2 · Finding
Finding

We're adding complexity to the task instead of making users' lives easier.

😬Why these bells and whistles?

The small window and extra buttons felt like overkill.

😬Other solutions are more convenient

Auto-populating in the browser already does the job for most simple cases.

Insight
AI delivers the most value when the task is complex.
“It's new, but it's not revolutionary. When it has original thoughts… that's revolutionary.”— Dylan, Copilot Pro user
“It's more of a partner than an assistant to me. I wouldn't use it this way.”— Brian, Copilot Pro user

Study 3 · Finding

Activities require more vs. less automation

🤖 AI should take the reigns a bit more. For tedious comparison and tracking work, users welcome higher automation.

💡 Users want ideas and suggestions. For discovery and inspiration, users prefer AI to offer options rather than act.

Participants chose one of: AI should complete all of this activity for me · AI should complete part of this activity for me · AI should only offer ideas and suggestions · AI shouldn't help me with this activity at all.

Scroll or hover to reveal the insight↓

Insight
Keep the fun in shopping.
“I enjoy browsing and discovering new things, especially when I'm shopping for something fun.”— User, 23, Texas
“Searching for the best deal is almost like being on a treasure hunt.”— User, 40, Illinois

Outcomes

Creating agentic AI experiences that support human decision-making.

Across five studies — from agentic consent and first-run, to shopping opportunities and journey continuity, to the Browser for Work — three threads held. Agentic AI earns trust by partnering with users on complex, multi-step tasks. Shopping demands user control because finances are sensitive and shopping is fun. Work calls for automation that handles what's behind the scenes while preserving how people connect with each other.
